What is the best ice cream flavor?

According to a survey by the International Dairy Foods Association, America's favorite ice cream flavor is chocolate. Here are the top 5 flavors among consumers.

  1. Chocolate
  2. Cookies and Cream
  3. Vanilla
  4. Strawberry
  5. Chocolate Chip

What about toppings?

Again, chocolate comes out on top! Chocoalte is the most popular topping in America. Other favorite toppings are...

  • Caramel
  • Sprinkles
  • Nuts
  • Cookies
  • Brownie Chunks
