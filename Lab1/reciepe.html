Katie's Pasta

Ingredients

  • 2 cups dry noodles
  • 1/2 cup pre-made pasta sauce
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 2 tbsp parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. In a pot, bring 4 cups of water to a boil. While waiting for water to boil, heat up the oil in a skillet.
  2. Once water is boiling, add in your dry pasta and let cook for 10 minutes.
  3. Sautee your onions in the skillet over medium heat. After 2 minutes, add in your garlic.
  4. When onions and garlic start to turn golden brown, add in your pre-made pasta sauce over low heat and cover.
  5. After 3 minutes, add your spinach into the skillet.
  6. Drain your pasta and pour it into the skillet.
  7. Mix your noodles inot the sauce.
  8. Plate and top with parmesan cheese. Enjoy!
